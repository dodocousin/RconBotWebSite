About me

Yes about me, i'm a solo dev !

Cousin704

Welcome to the world of BattleForge Commander RCON Bot! I'm a solo developer with a passion for video games and a drive to contribute to the gaming community. My journey began with creating tutorial videos on YouTube, sharing knowledge and tips with fellow gamers. Now, I've expanded my efforts to develop Discord bots, aiming to enhance the gaming experience for server administrators and players alike.

My mission

As a solo developer, my mission is to create powerful yet user-friendly tools that simplify game server management. By leveraging the popularity of Discord, I strive to provide seamless solutions that make administering game servers more efficient and enjoyable for everyone in the community.

Community-Driven development

My work is inspired by the needs of the gaming community. From my experiences creating tutorial videos to developing this Discord RCON Bot, every project is aimed at addressing real challenges faced by gamers and server administrators. I'm committed to continually improving and expanding the features of my tools based on community feedback and evolving gaming needs.

Future vision

Looking ahead, I envision creating a suite of tools that will revolutionize how we manage and interact with game servers. By combining my love for gaming with software development, I aim to contribute to a future where managing game servers is accessible, efficient, and enjoyable for everyone in the gaming community.
